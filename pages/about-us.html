About Us

Welcome to Ace Gifts Plus, your entry-point to a world where quality designer fragrances meet the very best prices in Canada. We fuse our industry expertise with our unique passion for perfumes to provide you with unparalleled customer service for all of your fragrance needs. It's what we call The ACE Advantage!

We thank you for taking the time to familiarize yourself with our company, our values and our fine products and services. On this page, we invite you to explore our company’s philosophy, be familiar with our business strategy and understand what we truly stand for.
 
Established in 1992, we are Canada's most trusted fragrance retailer, domiciled in the Greater Toronto Region. Our reach and clientele extends beyond borders, with customers ranging locally to customers spanning across Eastern and Western Canada.
 

To provide exceptional value and quality service, and deliver substantial price savings to all customers as Canada’s leading retailer of authentic brand name perfumes and cosmetics.
 

Our mission, as simplistic as it sounds in writing is something we take very seriously. To ensure our every action is aligned with our mission, we aim to add value to our clientele in every possible way. We hold desirable inventory levels and strive to ensure unrivalled customer service with our industry expertise and genuine desire to develop lasting relationships. 

Together with our values, fine reputation and industry experience, we are confident that  Ace Gifts Plus has more than what you want…we are what you’ve been looking for!
 
 
There's a reason thousands of loyal customer continue to choose Ace Gifts Plus as their number one choice for their fragrance needs. It's because our values are tied to the well being of our customers. We strive to ensure each customer receives the product they desire and the quality service they deserve!
 

Integrity and honesty in what we do

We believe every successful relationship rests on trust, honesty and respect. And we respect our customers' fundamental right to obtain uncompromised quality products. Since we have been trading for over 16 years, our customers know that we are consistent and reliable. We source all of our products from reputable suppliers that we know and trust. Being in the business for such a long period of time, all our customers know and have the confidence that we only trade in genuine products and maintain integrity in every aspect of our business. We take pride in our +3,000 brand name items, all of which are authentic and warehoused with due care. 

We have zero tolerance for fake or diluted items.

Our customer-first approach

Our customers are our most valuable asset and we understand that our success is tied to their well-being. Thus, we are committed to ensuring our customer's needs are met at every stage of the business transaction. This need-first approach is our company's differentiating aspect. Half the service areas in the city don't cater to the customer but make the customer cater to them. At Ace, we want our customers to be satisfied and endeavor to keep them more like friends. If there is a product that we do not have in stock or is warehoused at one of our retail locations, we will ensure to get it to the customer on a timely basis. We pride the excellence in our service!

 Long-term relationships

Nothing makes us more delighted than seeing our loyal customers return, even if its just to say "Hi!" At Ace, you will find a warm and welcoming staff ready to serve you and to provide you the best service in the country! We aim to maintain this close relationship and develop friendships with our valued customers during every step of the purchasing decision process. It's no surprise then, that we still have returning customers from near and far since our inception in 1992!
